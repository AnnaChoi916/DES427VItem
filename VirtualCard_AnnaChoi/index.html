🐝 Bee's Honey Hunt Game 🍯

  • 🌸 Move your mouse to guide the bee to the flowers.
  • 🖱️ Click on a flower to collect honey.
  • 🎵 Listen! You'll hear the bee buzzing and the sound of honey being collected.
  • 🍯 See how much honey you've collected in the honey jar on the top.
  • 🎉 Win the game by collecting honey from all the flowers!
  • 🎶 Enjoy the fun music while playing. But remember, the music stops when the game is over.
